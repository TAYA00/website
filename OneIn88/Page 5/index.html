HEAL provides support to organizations, camps, programs, and schools serving those with Autism Spectrum Disorders in Northeast Florida. Since 2007, HEAL has granted over $2.2 million in support of our local autism community. HEAL serves the Florida counties of Baker, Clay, Duval, Nassau and St. Johns.Your support of HEAL is greatly appreciated! Working together, we will continue Helping Enrich Autistic Lives in Northeast Florida!
